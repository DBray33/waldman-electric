A Century of Electrical Excellence

Waldman Electrical Contractors has been proudly serving West Reading and Berks County, Pennsylvania since 1918. For over a century, our family-owned business has delivered reliable, professional electrical services with a commitment to quality craftsmanship and customer satisfaction.

Our experienced team of electricians combines traditional values with modern expertise to provide solutions for all your residential and commercial electrical needs. From basic repairs to complex installations, you can count on our dedication to excellence and attention to detail.

Established 1918

With over 100 years of service to our community, Waldman Electrical has built a reputation for reliability, quality, and excellence in the electrical industry.

Expert Technicians

Our licensed technicians bring over 45 years of experience to every project, ensuring your electrical systems are installed and maintained to the highest standards.
