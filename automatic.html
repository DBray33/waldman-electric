Automatic Generators

The generators are permanently installed outside similar to an air conditioning unit. They work with a matched UL listed automatic transfer switch that responds within seconds when the utility power is lost. The transfer switch senses the loss of utility power and automatically starts the generator. Power is supplied to all pre-selected lights, appliances and equipment.

An important part of the system is the transfer switch that automatically responds when the power is interrupted.

When power is restored, the switch reverses the process and shuts down the generator.

Power When You Need It Most

Cummins Onan residential automatic generators, including the QuietConnect Series, provide homeowners with seamless backup power during utility outages. These systems automatically switch from utility power to generator power when outages occur and back again when service is restored—all without any manual intervention required.

Key Benefits:

  • Automatic Operation: The integrated transfer switch handles all power switching, allowing your home to stay powered even when you're away.
  • Whisper-Quiet Design: Engineered for exceptionally quiet operation with sound levels lower than typical conversation.
  • Smart Monitoring: Track performance and receive alerts through mobile apps, giving you peace of mind anywhere.
  • Cold Weather Ready: Specially designed to perform reliably even in harsh winter conditions.
  • Intelligent Load Management: Efficiently powers your essential home systems while optimizing fuel usage.
